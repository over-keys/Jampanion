FluidR3 GM SoundFont
FluidR3 GM is distributed by the Debian fluid-soundfont package. The package's
README identifies the SoundFont as released under the MIT license. Source and
license information: https://sources.debian.org/src/fluid-soundfont/3.1-5.2/
The Windows package contains the generated FluidR3_Jampanion.sf2 subset, which
retains only the General MIDI acoustic grand piano (program 0), vibraphone
(program 11, used by MIDI Thru), acoustic bass (program 32), and standard drum
kit (bank 128, program 0) needed by Jampanion.
